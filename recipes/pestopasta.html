Pesto Pasta

Description

Classic university dish i could rely on every week on rotation, super easy and cheap to make, available for both lunch and dinner either hot or cold which is another plus. Various options to spruce up the dish in addition

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken Fillets
  • Pesto
  • Crème fraîche
  • Sweet Peppers / Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in a pot of water
  2. Chop chicken fillets into small pieces and fry in a pan
  3. Chop sweet peppers and tomatoes and add to the pan
  4. Once pasta is cooked, drain and add to the pan
  5. Add pesto and crème fraîche to the pan and mix well
  6. Season with salt and pepper
